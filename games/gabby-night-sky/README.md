# Gabby Night Sky

An interactive webpage featuring a mysterious cat named Gabby roaming a beautiful, starry night sky.

You can interact with Gabby by clicking or tapping anywhere on the screen, and she'll walk toward you. If you bring her close enough to the top navigation bar, she might just be mischievous and knock it down! Don't worry though—there's a reset button to put everything back in place.
